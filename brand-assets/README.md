# Ellinas Brand Logo Assets & Style Guide

Welcome to the brand identity assets package for **Ellinas**. This folder contains official vector logo files in various formats and configurations, designed for premium web and print applications.

---

## 📂 Asset Files

1. **`ellinas-logo.svg`**
   * **Description:** Master color brand icon (Aegean dual-sail and waves with outer orbiting ring).
   * **Best For:** Profile pictures, icons, app icons, and minimal branding.
   * **Background:** Transparent.

2. **`ellinas-logo-with-text.svg`**
   * **Description:** Color brand icon combined with premium styled "Ellinas" typography and tagline ("PREMIUM TRAVEL").
   * **Best For:** Banners, splash screens, documents, and general header displays.
   * **Background:** Transparent.

3. **`ellinas-logo-white.svg`**
   * **Description:** Solid white monochrome version of the brand icon.
   * **Best For:** Dark background layouts, photo overlays, and dark-themed footers.
   * **Background:** Transparent.

4. **`ellinas-logo-dark.svg`**
   * **Description:** Dark navy blue (`#071c29`) monochrome version of the brand icon.
   * **Best For:** Clean white background layouts, letterheads, invoice templates, and light-themed print documents.
   * **Background:** Transparent.

---

## 🎨 Color Palette & Specifications

### 1. Aegean Tirkiz (Primary Accent)
* **HEX:** `#00f2fe` to `#00b4d8` (Gradient)
* **Usage:** Main wind sail, wave lines, brand text accent.

### 2. Egejsko Zlato (Secondary Accent)
* **HEX:** `#ffb703` to `#fb8500` (Gradient)
* **Usage:** Secondary wind sail (jib), subtle highlight elements.

### 3. Duboko Plava (Background & Text)
* **HEX:** `#071c29` (Deep Aegean Navy)
* **Usage:** Headers, dark theme backgrounds, dark monochrome logo version.

---

## ✍️ Recommended Typography

* **Title Font:** `Outfit` (Google Fonts) or `Montserrat` (Google Fonts)
  * *Weight:* Bold (700) or Extra Bold (800 / 900)
  * *Style:* Clean geometric sans-serif to mirror the sails' curves.
* **Body Font:** `Inter` (Google Fonts)
  * *Weight:* Regular (400) or Medium (500)
